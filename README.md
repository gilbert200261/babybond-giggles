# BabyBond & Giggles

BabyBond & Giggles is an AI-powered baby playtime idea app. It helps parents and caregivers find simple playtime activities based on a baby’s age, mood, available time, and activity type.

## Problem

Parents and caregivers may want simple baby playtime ideas but may not always know what activity to choose. They may also want ideas that are quick, safe, and easy to do at home.

## Solution

BabyBond & Giggles lets the user enter information about the baby, such as age, mood, available time, and activity type. The AI then suggests a simple playtime idea with supplies, steps, learning benefits, and safety reminders.

## Main Features

- Age-based playtime ideas
- Tummy time suggestions
- Music and reading activities
- Sensory play ideas
- Parent-baby bonding activities
- Quick 5-minute activities
- Calm bedtime play ideas
- Safety reminders

## Target Users

- Parents
- Caregivers
- Babysitters
- New parents
- Family members helping with baby care

## Disclaimer

This app gives general baby playtime ideas only. It is not medical advice. Babies should always be supervised during play.
