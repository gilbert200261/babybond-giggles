# Project Context

The project is called BabyBond & Giggles.

BabyBond & Giggles is an AI-powered baby playtime idea app. The purpose of the app is to help parents and caregivers find simple playtime activities for babies.

## Main Goal

The app should suggest baby playtime ideas based on:

- Baby's age
- Baby's mood
- Available time
- Activity type

## Output

Each activity suggestion should include:

- Activity name
- Supplies needed
- Step-by-step instructions
- Learning benefit
- Safety reminder

## Tone

Use warm, simple, beginner-friendly language. The app should feel helpful, calm, and parent-friendly.

## Safety

The app should not give medical advice. It should remind users to supervise babies during play and avoid choking hazards or unsafe objects.
